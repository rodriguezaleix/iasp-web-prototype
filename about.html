About IASP

The global community behind Science & Technology parks and Areas of innovation

IASP connects science parks, innovation districts and areas of innovation worldwide to drive growth, internationalisation, visibility and effectiveness for our members.

Join IASP Contact us
Our Mission

To be the global network for science parks and areas of innovation

Our mission is to be the global network for science parks and areas of innovation — helping our members grow, internationalise and become more effective in the ecosystems they serve.

We connect innovation communities across the world so they can share knowledge, build partnerships and create greater impact.

What We Do

Four ways we strengthen innovation ecosystems

Connect

We coordinate an active international network of science parks, innovation districts and areas of innovation.

Share Knowledge

We create spaces to exchange experience, insights and best practices between innovation ecosystem leaders.

Increase Visibility

We help our members raise their profile and strengthen their international presence.

Support Growth

We assist in the development of new parks, innovation areas and partnerships around the world.

Who We Are

A global community shaping innovation since 1984

Founded in 1984, IASP is an independent, non-profit, non-governmental association created to support the people and organisations shaping innovation ecosystems worldwide.

Today, IASP brings together science parks, innovation districts and areas of innovation from many different countries, with headquarters in Málaga, Spain and a branch office in Beijing, China.

Sustainability

The IASP Forest

As part of our commitment to sustainability and the UN SDGs, IASP collaborates with Treedom to support remote tree planting and help create positive environmental and social impact.

Every IASP member receives a tree as part of the IASP Forest — a growing symbol of our shared commitment to a more sustainable future.
